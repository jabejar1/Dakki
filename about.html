About us

Our prestigious Duel School is located in Heartland Island, sporting a scenic and naturalistic view! The mildly active volanic also ensures that those cold winters are a thing of the past! Our students are always kept up to date on the latest innovations in dueling, ensuring that students are always kept on their toes and learning new and interesting strategies to stay ahead! This doesn't mean that every student will be learn at the same pace, so we offer different tiers for learning!

  • Slifer Red-For those who are struggling in their studies, we offer extra help to help students try to catch up to their peers! We always believe in teaching those willing to learn, so just because you are an amazing duelist, does not mean you can't get there!

  • Ra Yellow-Those who have shown quite some promise and are very clear on their way to becoming a pro duelist! You clearly know your stuff, but you aren't at the top of your game, and need to work a little bit to reach your true potential!

  • Obelisk Blue-The very best of the best! Dueling comes naturally to these elite, they are the very best of the best and already posess a strong knowledge of the basics, and know many advanced techniques! There is no doubt these duelists will be talked about in the future and will become world class duelists with their abilities!
