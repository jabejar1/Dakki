Looking at the field

  • 1.Main Monster Zones
    • -This is where monsters are summoned, restricting a duelist to 5 monsters normally, unless a monster is special summoned from the extra deck.
  • 2.Spell/Trap zones
    • - This is where the spell and traps are played, or placed if being set face down for future use.
  • 3.Deck zone
    • - This is where a player sets their deck, your opponent has one on his side of the field as well!
  • 4.Graveyard
    • - whenever a monster is destroyed or a spell/trap effect is used, they go to the graveyard, not able to be used unless resurrected or brought back.
  • 5.Banish pile
    • - whenever a monster is banished, it is a lot harder to bring back, not always permanent but a lot harder to bring back!
  • 6.Field Spell zone
    • - whenever a player plays a field spell, it goes here! Each player can control their own field spell, and grants powetful effects.
  • 7.Extra Deck zone
    • - A player's extra deck monsters go here to be summoned during a game!
  • 8.Pendulum zones
    • - This is where players set their pendulum scales! So a player cannot use their leftmost or rightmost zones for spells or traps if they plan to pendulum summon
  • 9.Extra Monster Zones
    • - Link monsters, as well as pendulum monsters brought from the extra deck go here! Other extra deck monsters are not restricted like this!
