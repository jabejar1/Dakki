Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How many cards can a deck have?
As little as 40 or as many as 60!
Q: How many cards can an extra deck have?
None, or you can have up to 15.
Q: Can I summon more than once per turn?
One Normal summon only! But you can special summon as many times as possible!
Q: How do I summon from the extra deck?
Each type of card has its own way of summoning! Be sure to check each respective section to see how to summon each type of card!
Q: What's a special summon?
Any summon that is done by card effect, most cards will state if its a special summon. Also, any summon from the extra deck is a special summon.
Q: How do I enroll for a class?
Applications are always being accepted, and only those that pass a basic duel exam will be allowed to enroll.
Q: Is it hard to duel?
Not at all! Easy to learn, but difficult to master! Once you learn, your creativity and skill will be the only things holding you back!
Q: Is it hard to win?
Yes! Get gud scrub.
